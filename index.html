About Us

Welcome to PT. Widya Sarana Sejahtera

PT. Widya Sarana Sejahtera is a leading exporter of premium quality brown sugar. Our company is committed to delivering the finest brown sugar, sourced from the best plantations in Indonesia. With a focus on quality and sustainability, we ensure that our products meet the highest standards, catering to the needs of our global clientele.

Our mission is to provide superior quality brown sugar to our customers while maintaining sustainable practices. We strive to contribute to the economic growth of the local communities by promoting fair trade and supporting local farmers.
